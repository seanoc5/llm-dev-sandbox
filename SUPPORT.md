# Support

`llm-swarm-runner` is a sandbox / hobby project, maintained on a best-effort basis. There are no SLAs and no real-time support channels.

## Where to go

- **Bug reports and feature requests** — open a [GitHub issue](https://github.com/seanoc5/llm-swarm-runner/issues). Include enough detail (OS, Docker version, command you ran, what you expected, what happened) for someone to reproduce.
- **Questions, ideas, show-and-tell** — start a [GitHub Discussion](https://github.com/seanoc5/llm-swarm-runner/discussions) if enabled, otherwise open an issue tagged `question`.
- **Security concerns** — see [`docs/security.md`](./docs/security.md) for the project's threat model before filing; for anything sensitive, contact the maintainer privately via the email on their GitHub profile.

## What to expect

Responses are best-effort and may take days or weeks. PRs with a clear repro or fix are the fastest path to a resolution.
